        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>Proper Nesting: <b><i>Correct:</i></b> &lt;b&gt;&lt;i&gt;Text&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/b&gt;</li>

            <li>Elements in lowercase: &lt;p&gt;Text&lt;/p&gt;</li>
            <li>One Root Element: &lt;html&gt; ... &lt;/html&gt;</li>
            <li>Attributes in lowercase, quoted values, forbidden attribute minimization</li>
            <li>Image Alternate Texts: &lt;img src="blackcat.jpg" alt="a picture of a black cat"/&gt;</li>
            <li>Ampersands in URLs: &lt;a href="dataLibrary.php?page=12&amp;style=green"&gt;link&lt;/a&gt;</li>
